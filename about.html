About the club

OPC was formed in 1992 and is now one of UL's biggest clubs.The OPC's main goal is to spark the interest of our activities and to continue to develop the skills needed for those activities. We offer regular training for Caving, Climbing and Orienteering and major training for Mountaineering before international trips to Scotland, the Alps, and beyond! Head to ULWolves.ie to sign up and see some of our upcoming events!

Our Sunday trips are usually either hiking or rock climbing and no prior experience is needed. Just bring your boots and some waterproofs and you will be ready to go. For the rock climbing trips we supply all the gear including rock shoes, helmets and harnesses. On these Sunday trips you get a great opportunity to learn map skills or belaying and climbing techniques.

Hiking

Hiking is one of the most active sports in the OPC, with a hike taking place almost every Sunday and hikes on all of our weekend trips. They are a great place to meet other club members, enjoy some of the beautiful Irish countryside and of course be active! Our fantastic hike leaders are there to help you out and if you have any questions at all please email us on tripsulopc@gmail.com. See you out there!

Rock Climbing

Climbing is among the most addictive and exhilarating sports in the OPC. We have our on campus bouldering wall open every Monday Tuesday and Thursday from 7-10pm aswell as weekly trips to suas climbing centre on Tuesdays. The wall is a fantastic place to meet like minded people and make life long friends. Our more experienced members are also always on hand to help you improve. Outdoor climbing trips are dotted throughout the semester and usually take place on Sundays. We even run 3 international climbing trips a year. If you have any questions at all please email tripsulopc@gmail.com.

Orienteering

Orienteering is a competitive international sport that combines running with navigation. Its a timed race where you use a specially created, highly detailed map to find control points. The course difficulties are set to accommodate people of all abilities. Its a fun and unique way to experience the beauty of the Irish landscape.

Caving

Caving is an exhilerating way to see what Ireland has to offer. Few people see this part of the country, it's another world down there. We have regular single rope technique (SRT) training nights throughout the semester in preparation for caving, which is a great way to learn and meet some brilliant people while doing so.

Visit our climbing wall!
